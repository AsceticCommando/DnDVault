**The Raijin** (after the Japanese thunder god) is the generative force of
**collision** — worlds struck against worlds to make something neither could be
alone. Creation through violence and noise. A waking
[[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]] drifts toward the Raijin: better a destroying
storm than a kept silence.
