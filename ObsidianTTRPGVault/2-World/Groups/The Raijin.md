Arcs jump between distant curtains, fusing far-apart cosmoses for an instant and
birthing things that belong to neither — agents of monstrous novelty and prophecy.
May not want to conquer or save at all; it wants to **see what the next strike
makes**.

The waking twin [[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]] drifts naturally toward the
Raijin — the right to *change* over the fixed record.

## Politics
**Unaligned** with [[2-World/Groups/The Tehom.md|the Tehom]]. Opposed to
[[2-World/Groups/The Sijill.md|the Sijill]] on the **create ⇅ erase** axis.
