**The Tehom** (Hebrew *tehom*, "the deep") is **collapse and oblivion** — the force
that severs threads and drops them into the
[[2-World/Points of Interest/The Underdrop.md|Underdrop]], beyond record, rescue, or
recovery. A world lost to the Tehom is lost to *everyone*, including the Sijill's
archive. That irrecoverability is why all five other forces fear them.
