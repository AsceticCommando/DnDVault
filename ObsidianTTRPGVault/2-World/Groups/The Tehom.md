A fault line that *thinks*. Snaps curtains off the axis and drops them into the
**Underdrop**. Earthquake-as-divine-judgment (Korah; *tehom* = the primordial
deep). Possibly the oldest force; possibly trying to collapse everything.

## The linchpin (why all five others fear it)
A world the Tehom takes is lost to **everyone** — you can't annex, archive, freeze,
evacuate, or fuse a world that's been dropped. It is the closest thing to a
unifying threat, and the seed of the counterforce: the five can't openly unite
against it without admitting their own salvations are equivalent to its judgment,
so instead **one god broke ranks privately** (→ [[2-World/Groups/The Errata.md|the
Errata]]).

## Politics
**Unaligned** with [[2-World/Groups/The Raijin.md|the Raijin]] — refuses the whole
frame. Opposed to [[2-World/Groups/The Tethyne.md|the Tethyne]] on the **abyss ⇅
ocean** axis. Its debts are the only un-buyable ones (see the Ledger of the Six).
