**The Errata** ("the corrections") is the heresy born from
[[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā's]] crime: instead of *recording* the deaths of
worlds, he began *smuggling living ones out of the archive*. The Errata are the
smuggled — refugees, editions, and apostates who were never supposed to walk off the
page.

The party belong here in spirit. The faction's coming **civil war** (over whether
to keep saving editions or set the originals free) is a major late-campaign engine.
