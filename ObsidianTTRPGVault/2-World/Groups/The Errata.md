> [!warning] The counterforce — and it is *not good*.

"Corrections written into the margins of a doomed cosmos." Born of **treason, not
consensus**: it began when a single god, [[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]],
defected from [[2-World/Groups/The Sijill.md|the Sijill]] rather than from any noble
alliance — compromised at its root.

## Membership
Smugglers, cutters, evacuees, and defector-priests from all six faiths.

## Method
Smuggles living worlds and souls across the Hang **hidden inside Tethyne evacuation
convoys** (tidework as cover; see [[2-World/Hubs/The Slack-Tide.md|The Slack-Tide]]),
likely supplemented by a few Cuttings.

## The catch
Run by a god who still thinks in records and copies, the Errata may not save
*people* — it saves *versions* of people, and isn't always clear on the difference.
Even the rescue is also an ending. The party are its "corrections" (see
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|The Peripeteia]]).

## The fault line
The Errata civil war ignites when [[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]] begins to
wake inside the mask — the counterforce fractures between those who follow order
(al-Kutbā) and those who follow warmth/flux (Dushara).
