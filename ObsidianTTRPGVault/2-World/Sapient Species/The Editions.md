> [!note] Proposed extension — anchors the campaign's central reveal as an entity.

**Editions** are people smuggled *living* out of
[[2-World/Points of Interest/The Anthology.md|the Anthology]] — archived copies of
originals whose worlds were "saved" (i.e. recorded, then let die). Each carries a
**fidelity** rating: how perfectly it still matches the lost original. Fidelity
*drifts*. Low-fidelity editions misremember themselves
([[2-World/People/Oresh.md|Oresh]]); the most extreme drift is a copy *waking up* as
something new ([[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]]).

The player characters are Editions. This is the thing they learn in
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|the Peripeteia]] and must live with afterward.
