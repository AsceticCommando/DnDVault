**The Slack-Tide** is a refugee convoy — a caravan of linked "cars" riding the slack
water between threads of [[2-World/Galaxies/The Weft.md|the Weft]]. Crewed by
[[2-World/Groups/The Tethyne.md|Tethyne]] pursers, captained in spirit by the warm
"Reveler" the refugees follow. Home base for the party and the stage for
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|the Peripeteia]].

Key cars: [[2-World/Places/The Vintner's Car.md|the Vintner's Car]] (the sanctum) and
[[2-World/Places/The Tally-Car.md|the Tally-Car]] (the records).
