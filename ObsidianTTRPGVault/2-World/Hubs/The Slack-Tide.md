A **Tethyne tide-convoy**: a travelling community of barges and cars that rides the
tide between curtains, carrying refugees evacuated (and, secretly, *archived*) from
dying worlds. The party's home and the stage for
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|The Peripeteia]].

At the convoy's heart is the **Vintner's Car**
([[2-World/Places/The Vintner's Car.md|note]]), the sanctum of the rescuer the
refugees call "Dionysus." Pursers log every soul aboard in the **tally-car**
([[2-World/Places/The Tally-Car.md|note]]).

## Current beat
Anchored in a *lee* — a fold of calm — waiting out a Galewind raging two worlds
over. Shore leave. The rescuer has announced a feast in the party's honour at the
end of leave. The convoy is shadowed by the huntress
[[2-World/People/al-ʿUzzā.md|al-ʿUzzā]].

## Notable residents
- [[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]] — the patron, performing "Dionysus."
- [[2-World/People/Oresh.md|Oresh]] — a gentle weaver who is *almost* a person.
