> [!note] Proposed extension — the finale the whole tragedy bends toward.

Two pressures collapse together: **the brothers' war** for the shared body
([[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]] vs a fully-woken
[[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]]) and **the Errata civil war** — keep saving
editions, or set the originals free and let the copies end?

Endings to hold open, none clean:
- **The Kept** — al-Kutbā prevails; the editions live forever, perfect and unfree.
- **The Unwoven** — Dushara prevails; everything becomes living chaos again.
- **The Erratum** — the party author a third option the cosmos has no record for.
The point of a tragedy is that the audience sees the cost coming and cannot stop it.
