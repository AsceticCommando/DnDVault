The truth is out; now it has to be lived with. Do the party try to **free their
originals**, **destroy the archive**, **protect the other editions** (who don't know
what they are), or **side with the waking [[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]]**?

al-Kutbā's true name — a weapon that cuts both ways, since the party are his work.
Choices here set the alignment of [[2-World/Quests/The Exodos.md|the Exodos]].
