> [!info] Full GM module: **THE_WEFT_Peripeteia.docx** (filed in `1-DM Toolkit`).

A **two-session investigation module** (~6–8 hours): the party has been saved and
sheltered by a warm, vine-crowned god they know as "Dionysus." Across two sessions
they gather evidence that the rescue was something else, then force the mask off.

By the end they should know three things they can't un-know: they are **editions**,
not refugees; "Dionysus" is a mask worn by
[[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]]; and the face he wears belonged to his twin
[[2-World/People/Dushara.md|Dushara]], who is waking.

## The clue spine (not equal)
- Clue 1 — The Manifest That Counts Twice (DAMNING) · [[2-World/Places/The Tally-Car.md|the Tally-Car]]
- Clue 2 — The Refugee Who Remembers Wrong (DAMNING) · [[2-World/People/Oresh.md|Oresh]]
- Clue 3 — The Obituary With Wet Ink (EXONERATING) · [[2-World/Places/The Vintner's Car.md|the Vintner's Car]]
- Clue 4 — the huntress's warning · [[2-World/People/al-ʿUzzā.md|al-ʿUzzā]]

Clues 1 & 2 make the rescuer look like a predator; Clue 3 reveals genuine grief.
Only all three resolve the *tragic* truth: **a loving act that was also a crime.**
