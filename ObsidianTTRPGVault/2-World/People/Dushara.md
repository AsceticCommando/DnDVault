**Dushara** — Nabataean "Lord of the Mountain," vine and bull; ecstasy,
dissolution, living flux. The god of **things that change**. He is the twin
[[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]] archived alive and now **wears as a mask**;
the warm "Dionysus" face is his, performed by his brother.

## Fate: ANTAGONIST (locked)
Dushara was **imperfectly archived** — the copy is diverging from the original, and
the dissolution-god is **waking inside his own mask.** He claws back toward flux and
the right to change while al-Kutbā fights to keep the record fixed: a god at war with
the mask he wears.

He drifts toward [[2-World/Groups/The Raijin.md|the Raijin]] (collide & create) and,
at his darkest, toward [[2-World/Groups/The Tehom.md|the Tehom's]] collapse — better
oblivion than to be a kept edition again.

## Two endgame bosses in one body
The party may end up fighting al-Kutbā (who would keep them as perfect copies
forever) **or** Dushara (who would dissolve them, and everything, into living chaos),
**or** be caught between the brothers' war for the shared body. No version is safe.
