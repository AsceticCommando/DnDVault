**Dushara** — [[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā's]] twin and first "correction."
The warm face the convoy worships is *his*, worn by his brother. He was archived as a
proof of concept, and the flaw in every imperfect edition — the same drift that makes
[[2-World/People/Oresh.md|Oresh]] misremember her own home — is beginning to **wake
him inside the mask**.

## Two endgame bosses in one body
A waking Dushara drifts toward [[2-World/Groups/The Raijin.md|the Raijin]] (collide &
create) and, at his darkest, toward [[2-World/Groups/The Tehom.md|the Tehom's]]
collapse — better oblivion than to be a kept edition again. The party may end up
fighting al-Kutbā (who would keep them as perfect copies forever) **or** Dushara (who
would dissolve them into living chaos), **or** be caught between the brothers' war for
the shared body. No version is safe.
