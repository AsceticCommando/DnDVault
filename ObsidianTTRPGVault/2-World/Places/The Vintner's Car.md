A chapel-barge at the heart of [[2-World/Hubs/The Slack-Tide.md|the Slack-Tide]],
strung with living vine — the sanctum of [[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]] in
his "Dionysus" guise, and the site of the feast where
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|the Peripeteia]] confrontation lands.

**Clue 5:** the famous "living" vines never grow, fruit, or die. Cut one and it is
whole at the next glance — not healed, *reset*, as if the wound were never recorded.
A Dionysus who can make nothing grow is no Dionysus. Holds the reliquary scroll-case
with **the obituary with wet ink** (Clue 3 — exonerating).
