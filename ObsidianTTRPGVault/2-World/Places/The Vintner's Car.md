A chapel-barge at the heart of [[2-World/Hubs/The Slack-Tide.md|The Slack-Tide]],
strung with living vine — the sanctum of [[2-World/People/al-Kutbā.md|al-Kutbā]] in
his "Dionysus" guise. Site of the feast where
[[2-World/Quests/The Peripeteia.md|The Peripeteia]] confrontation lands.

**The tell (Clue 5):** the famous "living" vines never grow, fruit, or die. Cut one
and it is whole at the next glance — not healed, but *reset*, as if the wound were
never recorded. A Dionysus who can make nothing grow is no Dionysus. It is Sijill
preservation wearing Tethyne flux.

Holds the reliquary scroll-case containing **the obituary with wet ink** (Clue 3 —
the exonerating evidence).
