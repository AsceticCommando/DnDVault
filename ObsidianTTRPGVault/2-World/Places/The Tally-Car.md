Where Tethyne pursers log every soul aboard
[[2-World/Hubs/The Slack-Tide.md|The Slack-Tide]].

**The tell (Clue 1 — The Manifest That Counts Twice):** every rescued person is
logged *twice* — once as a living passenger, once under a second ledger in a Sijill
script no Tethyne crew can read. The second ledger calls them "entries," each with
a **"fidelity" percentage**. It reads like inventory. The crew don't understand it:
"the Reveler's own bookkeeping, we never touch it." (Plants the truth that the party
are archived editions graded for how perfectly they match their lost originals.)
