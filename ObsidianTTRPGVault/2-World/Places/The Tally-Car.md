Where [[2-World/Groups/The Tethyne.md|Tethyne]] pursers log every soul aboard
[[2-World/Hubs/The Slack-Tide.md|the Slack-Tide]].

**Clue 1 — The Manifest That Counts Twice:** every rescued person is logged *twice* —
once as a living passenger, once under a second ledger in a Sijill script the Tethyne
crew can't read. The second ledger calls them "entries," each with a **"fidelity"
percentage**. It reads like inventory: the party are archived editions, graded for
how perfectly they still match their lost originals.
