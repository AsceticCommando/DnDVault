# Mist Wanderer
*Source: RHW*  
![](file://backgrounds/RHW/Mist%20Wanderer.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom  
- **Feat.** A Dark Gift feat of your choice ([Mist Walker](3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/mist-walker-rhw.md) is recommended)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Survival](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Survival) and [Stealth](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Stealth)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** Choose one kind of [Artisan's Tools](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Artisan's Tools](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/artisans-tools-xphb.md) (same as above), [Lamp](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/lamp-xphb.md), [Oil](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/oil-xphb.md) (5 flasks), [Pouch](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/pouch-xphb.md), [Rope](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/rope-xphb.md), [Tinderbox](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/tinderbox-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), and 30 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

You once knew your home. But one day, the Mists rose and drew you into a Domain of Dread. Ever since, you have wandered between domains, braving the mercurial Mists as you search for a way home. Your time in these cursed realms has changed you, yet you find solace in communities of fellow wanderers throughout the Mists.
