# Vampire Devotee
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 6*  
![](file://backgrounds/ABH/Vampire%20Devotee.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Strength, Constitution, Charisma  
- **Feat.** [Vampire's Plaything](3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/vampires-plaything-abh.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Persuasion](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Persuasion) and [Stealth](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Stealth)  
- **Tool Proficiency.** [Cook's Utensils](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/cooks-utensils-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Cook's Utensils](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/cooks-utensils-xphb.md), [Fine Clothes](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/fine-clothes-xphb.md), 2 [Glass Bottles](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/glass-bottle-xphb.md), [Healer's Kit](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/healers-kit-xphb.md), [Perfume](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/perfume-xphb.md), [Lamp](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/lamp-xphb.md), [Oil](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/oil-xphb.md) (3 flasks), [Waterskin](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/waterskin-xphb.md), 19 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

You were in service to a vampire or a small group of vampires lairing together. The Undead drank your blood more times than you can count. You might have served willingly, perhaps with aspirations of one day becoming a vampire yourself. Or you might have been magically charmed and retain only a few muddled memories of your time as a vampire familiar. In either case, your time in the vampire den is over.
