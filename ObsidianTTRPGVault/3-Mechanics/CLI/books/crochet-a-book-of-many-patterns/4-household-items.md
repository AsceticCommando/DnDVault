# Household Items
*Source: Crochet: A Book of Many Patterns, p. 121* 

![](file://book/CaBoMP/095.webp#center)

There are few adventurers who don't appreciate the comforts of a well-appointed home, no matter how often their missions take them from its hearth. Indeed, the desire to protect their home has inspired many on their first quests and offers a joy that neither gold nor accolades can fully match. One's home is a form of everyday magic. No two are precisely alike, shaped by the needs, preferences, and whims of the folks who live there. The actual space can vary wildly, from the tiniest of nooks to grand halls suited to hosting hundreds. A home can be neat or cluttered, quiet or boisterous, an oasis of calm or a vibrant place that fills you with fresh energy. Yet in every form it is a place where you belong, exactly as you are.

Whether you are a [wizard](3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/wizard-xphb.md) who spends most of your time ensconced in your tower's library, a [cleric](3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/cleric-xphb.md) sharing a temple dorm with others of your faith, a [fighter](3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/fighter-xphb.md) whose fortress stands as a bulwark against raiding hordes along a war-torn borderland, or a [druid](3-Mechanics/CLI/classes/druid-xphb.md) whose simple cottage blends seamlessly with its natural setting, your home reflects your sense of self and the life you've chosen. The items within that home should similarly be unique, bringing a dash of your adventures to the space itself. Charming handmade pieces will remind both yourself and your visitors of the care and effort you have put into curating your space.

The projects in this section have been chosen to suit a wide range of homes. Most blend functionality with design and whimsy. Beginners will find endless uses for the Schools of Magic Granny Squares and the D20 Pillow, whose simple design can be incorporated into a wide array of patterns as your skills grow. The Mimic Dice Bag will appeal to the risk-taking side of every adventurer, while challenging you with new stitching techniques. Finally, the Soul Coin Coaster will add an infernal flair to your decor while keeping surfaces safe from unsightly stains. Each design will help contribute to a home filled with the warmth and magic that will bolster your bravery in dark moments and serve as a well-earned reprieve from danger.
