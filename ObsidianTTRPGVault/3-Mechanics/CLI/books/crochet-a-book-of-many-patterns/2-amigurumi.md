# Amigurumi
*Source: Crochet: A Book of Many Patterns, p. 11* 

![](file://book/CaBoMP/011.webp#center)

Walkway could be better than transforming fearful monsters and vile artifacts into cute and cuddly tokens of delight? That's precisely the magic you'll work with these adorable amigurumi patterns. These figurines are woven through with an enchantment that will appeal to adventurers of all ages and skill levels.

The realms offer an abundance of creatures whose likeness can be captured with yarn and hook. The chosen patterns cover a range of shapes and sizes, from the simple geometry of the Gelatinous Cube to the realm-hopping complexity of a Displacer Beast. After mastering these designs, advanced crafters might even want to try their hand at adapting these patterns to extend their repertoire. For example, other types of dragons might be created by adjusting the head frills and wing shape of the Red Dragon pattern—and changing the yarn color, of course.

Lest you dismiss this section as mere child's play, recall the usefulness of these amigurumi for any monster hunter. Few people have an adventurer's familiarity with the nuances of creature anatomy. Being able to provide a clear visual reference can be useful when determining the details of the threat you're being asked to confront. A beholder with only four eyestalks is merely a [spectator](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/aberration/spectator-xmm.md), for instance—still a threat, but a lesser one. Making sure that you're facing a [panther](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/panther-xmm.md) and not a displacer beast is well worth the effort of stitching up that pattern!

Indeed, [Volo Geddarm](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/volothamp-volo-geddarm-toa.md) claims to have met a Cormyrian monster hunter who handcrafted one amigurumi to represent each of the dangerous creatures that they had slain. It's worth noting that [Volo](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/volothamp-volo-geddarm-toa.md) was, at the time, selling a set of plushie toys purportedly from this collection, so the anecdote should be taken with a Volo-sized grain of salt. Even so, it illustrates one of the myriad uses an adventurer may come up with for the patterns in this section.

Once completed, you'll find that amigurumi are incredibly handy. They may serve as a cherished companion during solo quests or as a token of appreciation for your quest companions. They can be used to delight children, enhance a bardic performance, or distract a bored sentry. They can even become an offering of thanks to the unseen forces guiding you through your adventures. The choice is all yours.
