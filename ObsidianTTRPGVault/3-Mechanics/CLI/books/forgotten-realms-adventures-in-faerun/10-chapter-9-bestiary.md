# Chapter 9: Bestiary
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn, p. 249* 

![Xanathar—along with Sylgar, Ott Steeltoes, and the mind flayer Nihiloor—are only some of the many dangers adventurers encounter in the Forgotten Realms.](file://book/FRAiF/193-09-001.xanathar.webp#center)

Dangerous monsters and scheming villains populate the Forgotten Realms. This chapter presents numerous creatures that are particularly suitable for the regions described in this book.

The introduction of the "Monster Manual" explains stat block terminology and gives rules for various monster traits. Monsters are presented here in alphabetical order.
