# Lorwyn-Shadowmoor Origins
*Source: Lorwyn: First Light* 

![A Lorwyn boggart plays near an eclipsed realm](file://book/LFL/003-01-001.lorwyn-boggart.webp#center)

This chapter provides rules for backgrounds and species of player characters from the realm of Lorwyn-Shadowmoor. These elements suggest the characters' early experiences and family origins before taking up an adventuring life and learning the capabilities of a class described in the "Player's Handbook".

Heroes from the realm of Lorwyn-Shadowmoor might embark on the adventures described in "chapter 4", or they might find their way to another setting, at the DM's discretion.

## Backgrounds

Characters from the realm of Lorwyn-Shadowmoor can select one of the following backgrounds. Origin feats are described in the "Feats" section.

- [Lorwyn Expert](3-Mechanics/CLI/backgrounds/lorwyn-expert-lfl.md)  
- [Shadowmoor Expert](3-Mechanics/CLI/backgrounds/shadowmoor-expert-lfl.md)  

## Feats

These Origin feats are appropriate for characters from Lorwyn or Shadowmoor.

- [Child of the Sun](3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/child-of-the-sun-lfl.md)  
- [Shadowmoor Hexer](3-Mechanics/CLI/feats/shadowmoor-hexer-lfl.md)  

## Adapting Species

The following species are native to Lorwyn and Shadowmoor. Player characters of these species should use existing rules as noted in the descriptions below, while customizing a character's physical description and personality as desired.

- **Playing a Boggart.** Use the Goblin species (see [Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/goblin-mpmm.md)) when creating a boggart player character.  

        - [Boggart](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/boggart-lfl.md)    
- **Playing an Elf.** Use the Elf species (see the [Player's Handbook](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/elf-xphb.md)) when creating an elf player character from Lorwyn or Shadowmoor. For the character's Elven Lineage trait, select Lorwyn or Shadowmoor from the Elven Lineages table.  

        - [Elf](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/elf-lfl.md)    
- **Playing a Faerie.** Use the Fairy species (see [Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/fairy-mpmm.md)) when creating a faerie player character. Shadowmoor faeries have [Darkvision](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 120 feet.  

    For suggestions on how to run a faerie nonplayer character, see "chapter 3".  

        - [Faerie](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/faerie-lfl.md)    
- **Playing a Flamekin.** Use the Fire Genasi species (see [Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/genasi-fire-mpmm.md)) when creating a flamekin player character.  

        - [Flamekin](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/flamekin-lfl.md)    
- **Playing a Kithkin.** Use the Halfling species (see the [Player's Handbook](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/halfling-xphb.md)) when creating a kithkin player character. Shadowmoor kithkin have [Darkvision](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 120 feet.  

        - [Kithkin](3-Mechanics/CLI/races/kithkin-lfl.md)
