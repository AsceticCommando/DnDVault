> [!note] Definitions
> 
> **Adjective.** A word that *describes* something or someone  
> **Adverb.** A verb (action) usually ending in "ly"  
> **Noun.** A person, place or thing  
> **Plural Noun.** Multiple persons, places or things  
> **Verb.** Ab action, or something someone does  
^definitions
