# Adventure and Sidekick Ideas!
*Source: Muk's Guide to Everything He Learned From Tasha, p. 30* 

## Adventure Ideas

Every adventure in the Dankwood has a solution that can be attained without resorting to combat. Goblins can sneak out of danger, they can act all tough and bluff their way through a problem, and they are also known to be able to make friends with just about anybody (even [grumpy grung](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-grung-mgelft.md)!).

Read over the adventure hooks to get an idea of the problem or mystery and then check out the questions. These questions are prompts to let your players invent their own creative ways to solve the adventure and tell their own Dankwood story. There are infinite possible paths as to how each story can be told, so let your imagination lead the way!

### Tasha's Party

Tasha needs your help! She wants to throw a party and make her super special Green Bubble soup. Everyone in Gob-Town is excited but Tasha says to make Green Bubble soup she needs some gooey webs from the rare and elusive [spiderfrog](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md). Do you know where to get some for her?

You've heard that the [grung king, Robbit the Slimy](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/king-robbit-the-slimy-mgelft.md), might know where to get some—or he and [his grungs](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-grung-mgelft.md) may even have some webs to trade.

#### Questions to Ask:

- What does [King Robbit](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/king-robbit-the-slimy-mgelft.md) want in exchange for information about the location of the [spiderfrogs](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md) or (if he has them) for the webs? If you can find out what he likes, then you might be able to trade with him.  
- If the [grung](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-grung-mgelft.md) are too grumpy, then who would know where to find [spiderfrog](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md) webs in the Dankwood? Who would you need to ask to get this information? There are a ton of goblin friends who know stuff!  

> [!note] Ask Around Gob-Town! Find Stuff Out!
> 
> [King Robbit](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/king-robbit-the-slimy-mgelft.md) is shrewd and [grungs](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-grung-mgelft.md) are super grumpy. It's not going to be easy to get [spiderfrog](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md) webs from them.
> 
> Tasha knows that [spiderfrogs](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md) spin their webs next to clear, still ponds that reflect the moonlight. They also make a high-pitched squeaking sound during the full moon.

### The Lost Yeti

While wandering around the Dankwood, you found a [yeti tyke](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/yeti-tyke-mgelft.md). It's a mopey little critter but it's probably because it just misses its family.

You're pretty sure that its family lives in the Snowy Hills to the north. It's a long trip but if this [tyke](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/yeti-tyke-mgelft.md) is going to get back home, someone has to show it the way.

#### The Real Story!

The yeti tyke was separated from its parents by a group of [grumpy duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md). The yetis live in a cave, but that cave contains a secret door that leads to an underground labyrinth that has an ancient treasure hidden within it. The yetis didn't know about the secret door or the treasure but the [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md) sure did and they chased them out of the cave so they could hunt for the loot.

When the heroes return the [tyke](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/yeti-tyke-mgelft.md) to its parents, the yetis tell them about the [grumpy duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md) and how they've been chased out of their home. Can our heroes help get the yetis back in their home, give the duergar the boot, and maybe get some of that treasure the duergar are looking for?

#### Questions to Ask:

- Can anyone speak yeti? Maybe Tasha has a spell or a potion for that!  
- What are some possible ways to get rid of the [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md)? Some people who know about them say that [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md) are terrified of mind flayers—maybe the heroes could dress up like them and scare the [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md) away?  
- The yetis say that there is a super scary monster called a [remorhaz](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/remorhaz-xmm.md) living nearby. Maybe that monster could come in handy when dealing with the [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md)? It's risky!  
- Do the [duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md) have a map? That labyrinth sure is confusing and it could be easy to get lost in there!

### The Cursed Spring

The heroes come across a [pixie](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/pixie-xmm.md) who is crying by a spring that is all filled with stinky muck and bubbling sludge. If they ask what is wrong, she tells them that her beautiful spring has been cursed by an evil [sludge hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) that has moved into the Dankwood. She asks if the heroes can help get the hag to remove the spring's curse.

#### Questions to Ask:

- Can the [hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) be bargained with? Everyone has something that they covet or crave, maybe there's a way to find out what this [hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) desires above everything else.  
- Can you make friends with the [hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md)? Maybe no one has ever treated the [hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) with kindness—a fresh baked pie might do the trick!  
- Can the [hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) be tricked? Maybe they're a sucker for taking bets and, if you're good enough, you can beat them in a game of chance.  
- Can you use your friends to help? Your friend, Tasha, is a pretty powerful witch. Maybe she knows something about the [hag's](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md) weakness that you can take advantage of.

## Monsters

The following monsters have been simplified/modified to be used in Muk's Adventures. The following stat blocks are for your reference. Feel free to adjust or add in whatever you'd like to customize it to your story.

> [!embed-monster]- Dankwood Grung
> ![Dankwood Grung](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-grung-mgelft.md#^statblock)

Dankwood grungs come in a variety of colors and play different roles in their community:

- Green: Warriors, hunters and workers  
- Blue: Crafters and cooks  
- Purple: Leaders and commanders  
- Red: Scholars and magic users  
- Orange: Super elite warriors  
- Gold: The big boss  

> [!embed-monster]- Dankwood Duergar
> ![Dankwood Duergar](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/dankwood-duergar-mgelft.md#^statblock)

Dankwood duergar live in the deep caves below and are constantly on the search for treasure and magical discoveries they can claim as their own. Like their dwarven relatives, the Dankwood duergar are masters at crafting, but never seem to be happy or satisfied with their creations.

Some say that their constant pouting is what allows them the magical ability to double in size and strength for short moments. Now that's a temper tantrum!

![My treasure! Stony gray skin. White Hair.](file://book/MGELFT/00026c.webp#center)

> [!embed-monster]- King Robbit the Slimy
> ![King Robbit the Slimy](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/king-robbit-the-slimy-mgelft.md#^statblock)

King Robbit has a tiny pet snail whom he adores. Maybe if you bring the snail their favorite snack or toy, King Robbit will be willing to help!

> [!embed-monster]- Sludge Hag
> ![Sludge Hag](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fey/sludge-hag-mgelft.md#^statblock)

You can smell a sludge hag long before you see one. A super stinky smell like rotten eggs mixed with old flower water lingers whenever a sludge hag is around. Sludge hags also tend to leave oily, dark trails of goop around the locations they inhabit. Small critters who venture too close will become covered in the oily goop and require the help of another creature to get clean.

There are rumors that sludge hags were once pristine, fey creatures who became encased in gooey sludge after mistreating their environment. Another rumor is that sludge hags just have really itchy skin and the goop makes them feel relaxed.

![](file://book/MGELFT/00026d.webp#center)

> [!embed-monster]- Spiderfrog
> ![Spiderfrog](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/spiderfrog-mgelft.md#^statblock)

Spiderfrogs are unique to the Dankwood and may or may not have been created by the eccentric gnome wizard, Doonwaggle. Either way, their high-pitched squeaking sounds indicate that they are thriving in large numbers, despite being difficult to catch.

If you are lucky enough to come across an abandoned spiderfrog egg, raising one kindly will gift you the most helpful house-mate. Spiderfrogs are excellent at getting rid of pests and neatly pack away their webs at night, as they prefer to sleep under the shelter of a big, juicy leaf.

> [!embed-monster]- Yeti Tyke
> ![Yeti Tyke](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/yeti-tyke-mgelft.md#^statblock)

This family of yetis don't normally venture down to the Dankwood. Their home is in the frosty, Snowy Hills up north, where the air is cool and their fluffy-white fur makes for perfect camouflage.

A yeti tyke who has been separated from its family is scared, hungry and looking for a friendly face. Its loud, sorrowful howls will be a sure-fire way to locate where it's currently hiding.

To coax the scared yeti tyke out, you might try the following:

- Yetis have an amazing sense of smell and a smelly snack might do the trick!  
- You might not speak the same language, but some well-pitched yeti howls and yeti hand motions are sure to show that you mean no harm. (Make sure you show how it's done!)  
- This yeti tyke is missing its family, is there a way to disguise yourself as a long-lost yeti relative?
