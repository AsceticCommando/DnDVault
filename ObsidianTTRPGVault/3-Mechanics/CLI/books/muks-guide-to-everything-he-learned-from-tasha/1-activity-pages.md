A spell **COMPONENT** is what you need to cast a spell.

But they're not always items!

Sometimes it is a magical phrase or gesture.

What are some ingredients you might use for a spell?

> [!note] Types of Spell Components
> 
> ![](file://book/MGELFT/00010a.webp#center)
> 
> **Verbal (V).** Magical words that can be spoken, chanted or sung in a particular way.
> 
> ![](file://book/MGELFT/00010b.webp#center)
> 
> **Somatic (S).** Physical movements like waving your hands or wiggling your nose.
> 
> ![](file://book/MGELFT/00010c.webp#center)
> 
> **Material (M).** Particular objects that are needed to cast your spell.
^types-of-spell-components

> [!note] Definitions
> 
> **Adjective.** A word that *describes* something or someone  
> **Adverb.** A verb (action) usually ending in "ly"  
> **Noun.** A person, place or thing  
> **Plural Noun.** Multiple persons, places or things  
> **Verb.** Ab action, or something someone does
