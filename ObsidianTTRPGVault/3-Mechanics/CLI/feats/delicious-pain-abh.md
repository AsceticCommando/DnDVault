# Delicious Pain
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 9*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Toughened Flesh.** Immediately after you take Bludgeoning, Piercing, or Slashing damage, you can take a [Reaction](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to gain [Resistance](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage until the start of your next turn. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
