# Aberrant Anatomy
*Source: RHW*  

**Prerequisite**: Ravenloft Campaign

Exposure to alien horrors like those of the Far Realm has warped your physical form in supernatural ways. You gain the following features.

**Breathless.** You can hold your breath for 1 hour.

**Extrasensory Perception.** You have proficiency in the [Perception](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception) skill, if you lack it. You also gain [Expertise](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/expertise-xphb.md) in that skill.

In addition, you have [Blindsight](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Blindsight) with a range of 15 feet.

**Warping Flesh.** Immediately after you make a [D20 Test](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) and roll a 1 on the `dice:d20|noform|noparens|avg` (`d20`), the aberrant influence infecting your form flares, wrenching control of your flesh. Make a Constitution saving throw (DC 13 plus your [Proficiency Bonus](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)). On a failed save, you have the [Stunned](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Stunned) condition until the end of your next turn.
