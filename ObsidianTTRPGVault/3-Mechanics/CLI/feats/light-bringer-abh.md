# Light Bringer
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 10*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Sacred Magic.** You learn the [Light](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/light-xphb.md) spell and can cast it without Material components. If you already know that cantrip, you learn a different Cleric cantrip of your choice. The spell's spellcasting ability is the ability increased by this feat.

**Solar Luminance.** When you cast [Light](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/light-xphb.md), you can have the light from the spell be sunlight. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Sun's Healing.** As a [Bonus Action](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) while within sunlight, you can expend one of your [Hit Point Dice](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md), roll the die, and regain a number of [Hit Points](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
