# Echoing Soul
*Source: RHW*  

**Prerequisite**: Ravenloft Campaign

You experience echoes from a past or alternate life. You gain the following features.

**Channeled Prowess.** You have proficiency in two skills of your choice.

In addition, choose one skill you have proficiency in. You gain [Expertise](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/expertise-xphb.md) in that skill. Whenever you finish a [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md), you can change your choice for this benefit.

**Inherent Tongues.** You know one additional language of your choice, which you choose from the language tables in the Player's Handbook.

**Intrusive Echoes.** Immediately after you make a [D20 Test](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) and roll a 1 on the `dice:d20|noform|noparens|avg` (`d20`), memories and sensations from your soul's other life threaten to overtake you. Make a Constitution saving throw (DC 13 plus your [Proficiency Bonus](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)). On a failed save, you have the [Incapacitated](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition until the end of your next turn. While you are [Incapacitated](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) in this way, your [Speed](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is halved.
