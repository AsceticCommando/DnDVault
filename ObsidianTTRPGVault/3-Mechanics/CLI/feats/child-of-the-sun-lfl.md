# Child of the Sun
*Source: Lorwyn: First Light*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Eyes of Eirdu.** You and allies within 10 feet of you have [Advantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws made to avoid or end the [Blinded](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Blinded) condition.

**Faerie Fire.** You learn the [Faerie Fire](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/faerie-fire-xphb.md) spell. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat). You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have.

When you cast [Faerie Fire](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/faerie-fire-xphb.md) without a spell slot using this benefit, taking damage can't break your [Concentration](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Concentration) on the spell.
