# Niko's Mace
*Weapon ([mace](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/mace-xphb.md)), very rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  


- **Damage**: 1d6 bludgeoning
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

This Mace has 6 charges and regains `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the Mace, you can expend 1 of its charges to cast [Summon Celestial](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/summon-celestial-xphb.md) (`dice:1d20+9|noform|noparens|text(+9)` to hit with spell attacks).

*Source: Uni and the Hunt for the Lost Horn, Hold Back The Dead*
