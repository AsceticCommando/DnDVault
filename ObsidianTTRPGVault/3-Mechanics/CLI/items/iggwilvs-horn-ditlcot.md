# Iggwilv's Horn
*Wondrous item, instrument ([horn](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/horn-xphb.md)), rare (requires attunement)*  
![](file://items/DitLCoT/Iggwilvs%20Horn.webp#right)


You can use an action to blow this horn to cast one of the following spells from it: [Arms of Hadar](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/arms-of-hadar-xphb.md), [Fog Cloud](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md), [Gust of Wind](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/gust-of-wind-xphb.md), or [Web](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/web-xphb.md). If the spell requires a saving throw, the spell save DC is 13.

Once the horn has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn.

*Source: Descent into the Lost Caverns of Tsojcanth p. 14*
