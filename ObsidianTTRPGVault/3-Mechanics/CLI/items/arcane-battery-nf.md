# Arcane Battery
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  



This smooth, oval stone is etched with faintly glowing, magical symbols. While holding the battery, you can take a [Magic](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to touch the battery to one magic item. If that magic item normally regains expended charges daily, it immediately regains `dice:1d4+1|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4 + 1`) expended charges, and the battery loses its magic.

*Source: Netheril's Fall*
