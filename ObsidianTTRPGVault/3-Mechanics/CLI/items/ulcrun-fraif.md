# Ulcrun
*Adventuring gear, poison (injury)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 1,000 gp

A creature subjected to Ulcrun must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take `dice:6d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(21)` (`6d6`) Poison damage and gain 1 [Exhaustion](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 237*
