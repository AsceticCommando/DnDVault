# Mythallar Bracelet
*Wondrous item, common*  



Three small, crystal beads made from a decommissioned mythallar are strung on this leather bracelet. As a [Magic](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can pluck one bead from the bracelet to gain [Advantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Strength ([Athletics](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) checks for 1 minute.

A bead disappears immediately after it's plucked. Once all three beads have been plucked, the bracelet loses its magic.

*Source: Netheril's Fall*
