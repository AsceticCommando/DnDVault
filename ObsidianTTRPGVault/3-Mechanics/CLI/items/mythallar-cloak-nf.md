# Mythallar Cloak
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  



This electric-blue cloak is studded with sewn-in crystals that are shards of a decommissioned mythallar. The cloak has 10 charges and regains `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d10`) expended charges daily at dawn.

While wearing the cloak, you can take a [Bonus Action](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) and expend 1 charge to activate the cloak's magic. The magic lasts for 1 minute or until you end it early (no action required).

While the cloak's magic is active, you gain a [Fly Speed](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/fly-speed-xphb.md) of 30 feet and can hover. Additionally, once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with an attack roll and deal damage, you can cause the target to take an extra `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`) Radiant damage. If you are aloft when the cloak's magic ends, you fall.

*Source: Netheril's Fall*
