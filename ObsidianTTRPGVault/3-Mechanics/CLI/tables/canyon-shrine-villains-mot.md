# Canyon Shrine Villains
*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 154* 

**Canyon Shrine Villains**

`dice: [](canyon-shrine-villains-mot.md#^canyon-shrine-villains)`

| dice: d8 | Villain |
|----------|---------|
| 1 | A [minotaur](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/monstrosity/minotaur-of-baphomet-xmm.md) captures and devours other creatures limb by limb as part of a bizarre ritual honoring Mogis. |
| 2 | A tribe of [berserkers](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/berserker-xmm.md) ride [giant bats](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/beast/giant-bat-xmm.md) and hunt humanoids that travel through the canyon. |
| 3 | An [adult blue dragon](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/dragon/adult-blue-dragon-xmm.md) enslaves the inhabitants of the caves, intent on building an army to conquer a neighboring settlement. |

| 6 | A [lamia](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fiend/lamia-xmm.md) lures young nobles into its cult with promises of hedonism and sells those who disappoint it to other canyon inhabitants. |

| 8 | The victims of the canyon's inhabitants rise as [wraiths](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/undead/wraith-xmm.md) determined to end all life in the area. |
