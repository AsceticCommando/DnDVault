# Moonshae Druids Renown
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 185* 

**Moonshae Druids Renown**

| Renown Score | Reward |
|--------------|--------|
| 3+ | Moonshae druids and their allies are [Friendly](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 5+ | You can purchase [Potions of Healing](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/potion-of-healing-xdmg.md) from the druids for half cost. |
| 10+ | Beasts in the Moonshaes are [Friendly](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 15+ | You can purchase [Spell Scrolls](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/spell-scroll-xdmg.md) containing Druid spells of level 5 or lower from the druids. |
| 20+ | You are allowed to visit moonwells guarded by the druids and participate in their ceremonies. Plant creatures in the Moonshaes are [Friendly](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 30 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 30, the druids offer to take you to a moonwell and cast the [Commune](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/commune-xphb.md) spell—contacting the Earthmother—on your behalf. |
