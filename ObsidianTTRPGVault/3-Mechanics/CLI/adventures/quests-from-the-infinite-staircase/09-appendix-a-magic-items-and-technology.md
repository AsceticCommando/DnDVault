*Source: Quests from the Infinite Staircase, p. 190*

## Technology

This section presents technological devices and weapons that have special rules. The items are presented in alphabetical order. See the "Dungeon Master's Guide" for more information on "explosives and other futuristic weapons", such as [laser pistols](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/laser-pistol-xdmg.md) and [antimatter rifles](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/antimatter-rifle-xdmg.md).

On many D&D worlds, these devices are strange and unfamiliar anomalies, if they exist at all. If you're nervous about including such technology in your game, you can limit the number of [energy cells](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/energy-cell-xdmg.md) the characters discover during their adventures. Most devices are useless without them.

Due to their rarity, these items are priceless.
