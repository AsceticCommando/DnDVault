# Appendix B: NPCs
*Source: Heroes of the Borderlands* 

This appendix includes all the NPCs that the players may encounter in the adventures. They are presented in alphabetical order.

> [!note] Andrella
> 
> *Human Captain of the Guard*
> 
> - **Statblock.**  [Guard Captain](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/humanoid/guard-captain-xmm.md)  
> 
> This captain is strong, stern, and just.
> 
> - **Perfect Posture.** Andrella is as strict with her posture as she is with the law.  
> - **Role Model.** Many of the keep's young people admire Andrella and enjoy her goodhearted mentorship.  
> 
> - *"A dastardly bandit, Pral, accosts travelers along the trail. Apparently he's quite handsome; I'm sure he'll look even better behind bars." (True*)  
> - *"The castellan tells me monsters are drawn to the Caves of Chaos in droves. I wonder why." (True*)  
>

> - **Idiomatic.** Cornflower likes to use animal idioms, such as "busy bee," "chicken out," and "hold your horses."
