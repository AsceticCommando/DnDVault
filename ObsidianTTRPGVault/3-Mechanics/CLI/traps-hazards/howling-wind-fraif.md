# Howling Wind
*Environmental Hazard*  

Icewind Dale's winds can reach incredible pitch and volume, making it impossible to hear. Creatures outdoors in howling winds have the [Deafened](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 96*
