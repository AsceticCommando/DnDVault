# Charm of Electromancy

This Charm has 3 charges. You can expend 1 charge from this Charm to cast the [Telekinesis](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/telekinesis-xphb.md) spell. Once all its charges are expended, the Charm vanishes from you.

*Source: RHW*
