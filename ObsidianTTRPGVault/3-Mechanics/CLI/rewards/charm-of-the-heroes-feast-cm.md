# Charm of the Heroes' Feast

This charm allows you to cast the [heroes' feast](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/heroes-feast-xphb.md) spell as an action, no components required. Once used, this charm goes away.

*Source: Candlekeep Mysteries p. 128*
