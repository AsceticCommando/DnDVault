# Charm of the Black Rose

You can expend this charm to gain one of the following effects:

- You have [Advantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on ability checks made to influence [Lord Soth's](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/npc/lord-soth-of-sithicus-rhw.md) attitude for 24 hours.  
- You cast [Dissonant Whispers](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/dissonant-whispers-xphb.md) (level 5 version, spell save DC 17).  

The Charm vanishes once you use either effect.

*Source: RHW*
