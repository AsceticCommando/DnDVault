# Charm of Malediction

You can use the Charm as a [Magic](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to cast [Hex](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/hex-xphb.md) (level 3 version) or [Bestow Curse](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/bestow-curse-xphb.md) without requiring Material components. The Charm lasts for 7 days or until you use it. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the Charm's spells (choose each time you gain this Charm).

*Source: RHW*
