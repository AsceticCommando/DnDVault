# Blessing of the Lone Champion

You gain the following benefits:

- **Heroic Inspiration.** You gain <span title="Player's Handbook (2024)">Heroic Inspiration</span> when you finish a <span title="Player's Handbook (2024)">Short</span> or <span title="Player's Handbook (2024)">Long Rest</span> and whenever the DM has you roll Initiative.  
- **Temporary Hit Points.** You gain a number of <span title="Player's Handbook (2024)">Temporary Hit Points</span> equal to 10 times your level when you gain this blessing and when you gain a level.  

*Source: Dragon Delves p. 6*
