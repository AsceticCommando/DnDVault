# Blasphemous Charm
*Charm*  

You can use this Charm as a [Magic](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to cast [Hunger of Hadar](3-Mechanics/CLI/spells/hunger-of-hadar-xphb.md) without requiring Material components. You can't gain this Charm again while you have it.
