# Lupin
*Source: RHW*  
![](file://races/RHW/Lupin.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores**: None
- **Type**: humanoid
- **Size**: Small or Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft.

## Traits

### Darkvision

You have [Darkvision](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/senses.md#Darkvision) with a range of 60 feet.

### Feral Pounce

Your [Unarmed Strikes](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) deal Slashing damage instead of Bludgeoning damage. In addition, when you hit a creature with an [Unarmed Strike](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) as part of the [Attack](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Attack) action on your turn, you can use both the [Damage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/damage-xphb.md) and the Shove options. You can use this benefit only once per turn.

### Howl

As a [Bonus Action](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you let out an unearthly howl. Each creature of your choice within 15 feet of you must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 plus your Constitution modifier and [Proficiency Bonus](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)) or have [Disadvantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls and saving throws until the start of your next turn.

You can use this trait a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

### Werewolf Instincts

You have proficiency in the [Perception](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Perception), [Stealth](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Stealth), or [Survival](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/skills.md#Survival) skill.

## Description

With their bristling fur and snarling muzzles, lupins cast imposing shadows in the Mists. Lupins often arise from encounters with werewolves in which the curse of lycanthropy didn't fully take hold. Unlike true werewolves, lupins can't shift between wolf and humanoid form but are instead stuck as a hybrid transformation. This cursed wolflike physiology affords lupins bestial might, enhanced instincts, and a dread-inducing howl.
