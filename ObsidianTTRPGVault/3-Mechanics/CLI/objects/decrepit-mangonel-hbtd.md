# Decrepit Mangonel
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*Source: Hold Back The Dead p. 5*  

Loading a Decrepit Mangonel requires two [Utilize](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) actions, and aiming it requires one [Utilize](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action. Then a creature can take the Sack of Hands action.

## Actions

***Sack of Hands (Requires Load and Aim).*** A launched sack of writhing hands lands in a space 30 to 100 feet away from the mangonel. A [Swarm of Crawling Claws](3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/undead/swarm-of-crawling-claws-xmm.md) appears where the sack lands.
