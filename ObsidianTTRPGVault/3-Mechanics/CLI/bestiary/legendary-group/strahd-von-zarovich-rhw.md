# Strahd von Zarovich

## Regional Effects


Barovia is altered by Strahd's presence, creating the following effects:

- **Closing the Borders.** While in his domain, Strahd can close or open the borders to Barovia at will. While the borders are closed, noxious fog suffuses the Mists surrounding Barovia. A creature that enters the Mists for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw or gain 1 [Exhaustion](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level. A creature makes this save only once per turn.  
- **Dreadful Darkness.** While in an area of [Darkness](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/darkness-xphb.md), creatures other than Strahd and creatures of his choice have [Disadvantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to end or avoid the [Frightened](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition.  
- **Jealous Heart.** When a character receives [Heroic Inspiration](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md) but already has it, that character can't give it to another character in the group.  

If Strahd dies, these effects end immediately.
