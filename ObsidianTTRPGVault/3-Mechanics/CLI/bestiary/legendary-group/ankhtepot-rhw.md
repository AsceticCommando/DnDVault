# Ankhtepot

## Regional Effects


Har'Akir is altered by Ankhtepot's presence, creating the following effects:

- **Blistering Sun.** Any outdoor area of [Bright Light](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in Har'Akir is an area of [extreme heat](3-Mechanics/CLI/traps-hazards/extreme-heat-xdmg.md) (see the "Dungeon Master's Guide").  
- **Closing the Borders.** While in his domain, Ankhtepot can close or open the borders to Har'Akir at will. While the borders are closed, whirling sands fill the Mists surrounding Har'Akir. A creature that enters the Mists for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there takes `dice:2d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d6`) Slashing damage.  
- **Control Undead.** Undead of CR 5 or lower in Har'Akir have the [Charmed](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) condition and obey Ankhtepot's orders.  

If Ankhtepot dies, these effects end immediately.
