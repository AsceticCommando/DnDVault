# Viktra Mordenheim

## Regional Effects


Lamordia is altered by Viktra's presence, creating the following effects:

- **Air of Obsession.** When a creature fails a Constitution saving throw to maintain [Concentration](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#Concentration), that creature takes `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(3)` (`1d6`) Psychic damage.  
- **Closing the Borders.** While in her domain, Viktra can close or open the borders to Lamordia. While the borders are closed, lightning storms roll through the Mists surrounding Lamordia. A creature that enters the Mists for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there makes a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) Lightning damage on a failed save and half as much on a successful one. A creature makes this save only once per turn.  

If Viktra dies, these effects end immediately.
