# Chakuna

## Regional Effects


Valachan is altered by Chakuna's presence, creating the following effects.

- **Closing the Borders.** While in her domain, Chakuna can close or open the borders to Valachan at will. While the borders are closed, shadowy beasts skulk or soar amid the Mists surrounding Valachan. A creature that enters the Mists for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d10`) Piercing damage.  
- **Heightened Senses.** Beasts and Monstrosities have [Advantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Wisdom checks.  
- **Whispering Shadows.** Humanoids subtract `dice:1d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d8`) from any ability checks they make when they take the [Hide](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Hide) action.  

If Chakuna dies, these effects end immediately.
