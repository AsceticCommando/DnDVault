# Toll the Dead
*cantrip, Necromancy*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You point at one creature you can see within range, and the single chime of a dolorous bell is audible within 10 feet of the target. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take `dice:1d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d8`) Necrotic damage. If the target is missing any of its [Hit Points](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), it instead takes `dice:1d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d12`) Necrotic damage.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by one die when you reach levels 5 (`dice:2d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d8`) or `dice:2d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d12`)), 11 (`dice:3d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d8`) or `dice:3d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d12`)), and 17 (`dice:4d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d8`) or `dice:4d12|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d12`)).
