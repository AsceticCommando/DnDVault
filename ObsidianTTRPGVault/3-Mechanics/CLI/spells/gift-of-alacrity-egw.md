# Gift of Alacrity
*1st-level, Divination*  


- **Casting time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hours

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add `dice:1d8|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d8`) to its initiative rolls.
