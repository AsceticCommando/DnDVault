# Guiding Bolt
*1st-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

You hurl a bolt of light toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, it takes `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) Radiant damage, and the next attack roll made against it before the end of your next turn has [Advantage](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md).

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(1d6)` for each spell slot level above 1.
