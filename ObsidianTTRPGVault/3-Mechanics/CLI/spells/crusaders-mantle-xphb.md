# Crusader's Mantle
*3rd-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

You radiate a magical aura in a 30-foot [Emanation](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). While in the aura, you and your allies each deal an extra `dice:1d4|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d4`) Radiant damage when hitting with a weapon or an [Unarmed Strike](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md).
